Describe a public park or garden
You should say:

  • Where it is
  • What it is like
  • How often you go there
  • What you often do there

And explain how you feel about it

  • Where it is
    它位于北京，实际上是位于北京的海淀区，作为中国的首都，这里离我的大学非常近，大约两公里的距离，所以我常常步行过去，大概需要半小时。
    It is located in Beijing, actually it is located in Haidian District, Beijing, the capital of China. It’s quite close to my univeristy, about two kilometers away, so it often takes me about half an hour to walk there.
  • What it is like
    它像一个典型的公园，拥有你所期望的所有常见设施。这里有步道、开阔的绿地，还有很多长椅供人坐下来休息。周围被树木和花朵环绕，这让空气感觉清新，环境也非常宁静。它是一个简单却非常适合放松并享受大自然的好地方。
    It’s like a typical park with all the usual features you’d expect. There are walking paths, open green spaces, and plenty of benches to sit and relax. It’s surrounded by trees and flowers, which makes the air feel fresh and the environment peaceful. It’s a simple but great place to unwind and enjoy nature.
  • How often you go there
    我去那里很频繁，因为它离我的学校很近。每当我需要休息或想散步时，去那里都非常方便。大多数时候，我都会尝试花些时间去那里放松。
    I go there quite often since it’s very close to my school. It’s convenient for me to drop by whenever I need a break or want to take a quick walk. On most days, I try to spend at least some time there to relax.
  • What you often do there
    我经常沿着被树木和花朵围绕的小径散步。清新的空气总是能让我感到焕然一新，并清理我的思绪。每当我想要放松并逃离日常生活的压力时，这里就是我首选的地方。
    I often go walking along the paths surrounded by trees and flowers. The fresh air always helps me feel refreshed and clear my mind. It’s my go-to place when I want to relax and escape from the stress of daily life.
  • explain how you feel about it
    我真的很喜欢这个地方，因为它非常宽敞，我是说，步道宽阔开阔，非常适合散步。我非常享受这里，所以我相信将来我还会再来。
    I really like the place because it is very spacious, I mean, the paths are wide and open, just perfect for walking. I enjoy it so much that I’m sure I’ll visit again in the future.

You should say:

  • Where it is
  • What it is like
  • What you often do there
  • Who you often go there with

And explain why you think it is different

    It is located in Beijing, actually it is located in Haidian District, Beijing, the capital of China. It’s quite close to my univeristy, about two kilometers away, so it often takes me about half an hour to walk there.
  • What it is like
    It’s like a typical park with all the usual features you’d expect. There are walking paths, open green spaces, and plenty of benches to sit and relax. It’s surrounded by trees and flowers, which makes the air feel fresh and the environment peaceful. It’s a simple but great place to unwind and enjoy nature.
  • What you often do there
    I often go walking along the paths surrounded by trees and flowers. The fresh air always helps me feel refreshed and clear my mind. It’s my go-to place when I want to relax and escape from the stress of daily life.
  • Who you often go there with
    I usually go to the park with my family. We like to take walks together, especially after dinner or on weekends. It’s a relaxing way to spend quality time as a family. Sometimes, I also go with my best friend. The park is a popular place to meet up, so whenever someone wants to catch up, it’s the perfect spot.

喜欢一起聊天的朋友

Describe a friend you like to talk with
You should say:
-Who he/she is
-What you like to talk about
-Why you like to talk with him/her
And explain how you feel about him/her

-Who this person is
他是我的邻居。他是一个非常有趣且博学的人，特别喜欢读书和分享知识。
-What you like to talk about
我们经常聊一些和书籍相关的话题并分享各自的感受，比如最近阅读的书籍观点和人物。有趣的是，他总能用生活中的一些小故事把这些书的内容生动地呈现出来，让我觉得津津有味。
-Why you like to talk with him/her
我喜欢和他聊天，因为他知识面广，能把一些枯燥的内容说的更加有吸引力。此外，他家离我很近，我可以随时去找他聊天，彼此分享有趣的想法。
-explain how you feel about him/her
我觉得他不仅是个有趣的人也是个思想深刻的人。我非常珍惜和他交流的时间，因为每次都能带给我启发。

-What he/she likes to read
他喜欢读的书涵盖了多个领域，尤其是冒险类书籍，例如哈利波特。
-why you think he/she likes to read a lot
我觉得他读很多书的原因是他想通过书籍探索未知领域，来增加生活的乐趣。

想要共事的家人

Describe a family member who you want to work with in the future
You should say:
-Who he/she is
-Whether you worked together before
-What kind of work you would like to do with him/her
And explain how you feel about this family member

-Who he/she is
他是我的祖父，今年有70岁高龄，是一位充满活力的老人。虽然年纪较大，但他依旧像年轻人一样忙碌和热情地生活着。
-Whether you worked together before
是的，我之前和他一起工作过。当我在他的农场里，帮忙摘玉米。工作完之后，祖父给我做了美味的烤玉米，那是我难忘的记忆。
-What kind of work you would like to do with him/her
未来，我希望能够通过我的技术知识和祖父的工作经验来创造农业机器，为他简化劳动强度，同时提高农场产量。
-explain how you feel about this family member
我非常崇拜他，我认为不仅是一位勤奋的农民，更是一个生活的智者。我希望自己的生活以后能够像他一样，保持乐观的心态，并努力去创造属于自己的精彩人生。

社交媒体上常刷的人

Describe a person who you follow on social media
You should say:
-Who he/she is
-How you knew him/her
-What he/she posts on social media
And explain why you follow him/her on social media

OK, Well, I am going to talk about a person, who is called Jay Chou.
-Who he/she is
他是在我们国家非常著名的歌手和音乐制作人, 他的作品风格融合了流行和中国传统元素，吸引了很多人，尤其是年轻人。
-How you knew him/her
我第一次知道他在我高中音乐课上，老师向我们讲述音乐知识，并以他的歌曲为例，当时我就被他的风格深深吸引，自那之后，他的音乐就成为了我生活的一部分
-What he/she posts on social media
他经常在他的社交媒体上分享关于他专辑和演唱会的动态，深受国内外粉丝喜爱。他还会发一些日常生活的点滴，显得他很平易近人。
-why you follow him/her on social media
我关注他的社交媒体因为他的音乐充满深意，能够与我产生共鸣，同时我也非常敬佩他的勤奋以及对音乐的执着。

你的国家的名人

Describe a popular/well-known person in your country
You should say:
-Who this person is
-What he/she has done
-Why he/she is popular
And explain how you feel about this person

OK, Well, I am going to talk about a person, who is called Jay Chou.
-Who he/she is
他是在我们国家非常著名的歌手和音乐制作人, 他的作品风格融合了流行和中国传统元素，吸引了很多人，尤其是年轻人。
-What he/she has done
他发布了很多畅销专辑和流行歌曲，并且赢得了国内外很多奖项，最大的成就之一是将中国元素融入到音乐中，向世界推广中国文化。
-Why he/she is popular
周杰伦因为他的创造力、音乐风格而受欢迎。许多人不仅欣赏他的才华，还敬佩他的勤奋和对音乐的执着。他的歌曲往往充满深意，与不同年龄段听众产生共鸣，给他们留下深刻印象。
-how you feel about this person
我真的非常喜欢周杰伦，从高中开始，他的音乐就成为了我生活的一部分，他们给我带来了很多安慰。
