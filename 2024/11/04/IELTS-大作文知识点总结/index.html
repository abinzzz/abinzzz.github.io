雅思大作文写作技巧总结

1.分类

议论文：

  • To what extent do you agree or disagree?
  • Discuss both views and give your opinion.
  • Do you think the advantage of this develoment outweigh the disadvantages?
  • Do you think this is a positive or a negative development?

—

报告文：

  • What is the reason, and what can be done to encourage a positive attitude to learning?
  • What are the reasons? What are the effects on society and the family?
  • What problems does it cause? How can we solve these problems?

2. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

结构：

  • Some suggest that…
  • To begin with, …65-85-100(如果不写反驳段，需要扩充到100字)
  • In addition, ….65-85-100
  • *反驳段….65-85
  • In conclusion, I firmly believe that …….This not only 复述分论点1 but also复述分论点2

—

中间段：

  • 分论点
  • 前提背景
  • 解释
  • 小推导

travel

Parents should encourage their children to spend less time on studying and more time on physical activities. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

开始段：Some suggest that mothers and fathers ought to motivate motive their offspring to allocate more time to physical pursuits instead of academic learning. spend less time on studying than on physical activities. However, I strongly disagree with this perspective for several reasons.

—

旅游的好处：

  • 旅游能拓展孩子们的视野
  • 旅游能使孩子们交到更多的朋友
  • 旅游能提升孩子们解决问题的能力
  • 旅游能使人们放松

—

例子：

  • 分论点1：旅游A能使人们放松B
  • 前提背景：In today’s competitive academic landscape, 很多孩子面临着巨大的压力，导致一些心理健康问题比如说焦虑。
  • 解释：当人们旅游的时候，他们有机会尝试各种各样的娱乐活动，比如看风景，享受美食，参与当地节日，拍美照等等，这能让他们享受活动带来的快乐以及从学习压力中解脱出来，这能他们放松。
  • 小推导：这种放松不仅能让他们以更积极的心态对待接下来的学习还能提高他们的心理健康。

When/By/Through A-A1-A2-A3-A4-A5-B-B1

—

Volcabulary:

  • Encourage-inspire-motivate-stimulate
  • Spend-allocate-dedicate-devote…to sth/doing sth
  • Physical activities-Physical pursuits
  • Outdoor pursuits
  • Studying -academic learning
  • Bring up-raise
  • Children-offspring

范文分析

外国游客是否应该比本地游客花更多钱

Foreign visitors should pay more than local visitors for cultural and historical attractions.
To what extent do you agree or disagree with this opinion?

—

反方观点：景点需要通过国家补贴来维持运行-补贴来源于当地人交的税-当地人已经交过了税，而外国人没有交税-外国人需要收更多的钱

正方观点：如果提高价格会抑制外国游客来本地-经济的贡献下降-景点维护费用不足-外国人不需要交更多的钱

外国游客消费 -> 增加经济收入 -> 支持旅游业发展 -> 促进文化遗产维护 -> 吸引更多游客

选择工作最重要的考虑因素是薪水么

When choosing a job, the salary is the most important consideration. To what extent do you agree or disagree? 部分同意:同意salary重要，其他也一样重要

人们会追求收入高的工作-》钱能满足各种需求-》提升生活质量

其他因素同样重要：

  • 办公室的氛围
  • 社会贡献感

青少年是否应该无偿为社区工作

Some people think that all teenagers should be required to do unpaid work in their free time to help the local community. They believe this would benefit both the individual teenager and society as a whole.

Do you agree or disagree?

学生需要每天上课-》学习的压力已经很大-》不应该去社区工作，而是说更多的时间和朋友享受时光，体育活动

强迫工作-》不满情绪-》不会有积极的结果

我们应该只关心自己的国家么

We cannot help everyone in the world that needs help, so we should only be concerned with our own communities and countries.

To what extent do you agree or disagree with this statement?
